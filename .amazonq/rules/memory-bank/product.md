# Product Overview

## Project Purpose
Core_system is a comprehensive multi-tenant Laravel-based CMS and e-commerce platform designed to manage content, products, orders, projects, employees, and contracts. It provides a complete business management solution with advanced features for content management, e-commerce operations, project tracking, and employee management.

## Key Features and Capabilities

### Content Management System (CMS)
- Multi-tenant architecture supporting multiple branches and websites
- Dynamic page builder with widget system
- Menu and navigation management
- Post and page management with SEO optimization
- Media library integration with Spatie Media Library
- Form submission handling
- Visitor logging and analytics

### E-commerce Platform
- Product catalog with categories, brands, and attributes
- Product variations and attribute mapping system
- Shopping cart and checkout functionality
- Order management with status tracking
- Product reviews and ratings
- Inventory management
- SEO-optimized product pages

### Project Management
- Project creation and tracking
- Task management system
- Project permissions and access control
- Project tickets and issue tracking
- Activity logging for audit trails
- Project settings and customization

### Employee & Contract Management
- Employee profiles with roles and departments
- Contract management with account fields
- Department and manager assignments
- User level-based access control (superadmin, admin, employee)
- Employee-project associations

### Widget System
- Extensible widget architecture (BaseWidget, HTMLWidget, ProductListWidget)
- Pre-built widgets: Hero, Banner, Slider, Category, Product, Brand, News, Post, About, Marketing
- Widget templates and registry
- Dynamic widget rendering on pages

### Multi-tenant Support
- Tenant isolation across all tables
- Branch management for multi-location businesses
- Tenant-specific settings and configurations

## Target Users and Use Cases

### Primary Users
- **Business Owners**: Manage multiple websites, branches, and e-commerce operations
- **Content Managers**: Create and publish content, manage pages and posts
- **E-commerce Managers**: Handle product catalogs, orders, and customer reviews
- **Project Managers**: Track projects, assign tasks, manage team members
- **HR Managers**: Manage employees, contracts, and organizational structure
- **Developers**: Extend functionality through widgets and custom modules

### Use Cases
- Multi-brand e-commerce websites with centralized management
- Corporate websites with project and employee management
- Content-driven platforms with dynamic page building
- Multi-location businesses requiring branch-specific content
- Organizations needing integrated CMS, e-commerce, and project management
- Businesses requiring tenant isolation for multiple clients
