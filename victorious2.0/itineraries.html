Itinerary detail

DAY 1
PEARL FARM TUNG SAU - TITOP ISLAND – KAYAK

08:00 – 8:30: Pick up at your hotel in Hanoi Old Quarter. Start 2.5 hours road journey on shuttle limousine bus (for the guests use our transfer service).

If you go to Halong Bay on your own, please be ready at Victorius Cruise’s Waiting area Tuan Chau International Marina, Halong Bay  at 11:30 – 11:45 A.M for check-in. You will be transferred to Victorius Cruise from the tender.

DAY 2
SURPRISE CAVE

08:00 – 8:30: Pick up at your hotel in Hanoi Old Quarter. Start 2.5 hours road journey on shuttle limousine bus (for the guests use our transfer service).

If you go to Halong Bay on your own, please be ready at Victorius Cruise’s Waiting area Tuan Chau International Marina, Halong Bay  at 11:30 – 11:45 A.M for check-in. You will be transferred to Victorius Cruise from the tender.

INCLUSIONS
  • Luxury accommodation with modern amenities, including a bathtub and a private balcony.
  • 01 lunch / 01 dinner / 01 breakfast / 01 brunch.

  • Complimentary use of the swimming pool, gym and kayak.
  • Welcome fresh fruits upon check-in.

  • Activities onboard (cooking class, squid fishing...).

  • Taxes and service charges.
  • Shared tour guide.

  • Food and beverage not included in the room rates.
  • Massage, spa, and beauty services.
  • Tips for staff and other service charges included in the room rate.
